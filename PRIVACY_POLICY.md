# Privacy Policy for Prism

Effective date: 2026-04-26

Prism is a browser new-tab extension. This Privacy Policy explains what information Prism accesses, how it is used, and your choices.

## Overview

Prism is designed to be privacy-first:

- No account is required.
- No personal data is sold.
- No analytics, tracking SDK, or advertising SDK is included.
- Prism does not operate a backend server that collects your extension data.

## Information Prism Accesses

Prism requests only the permissions needed for its features:

- `bookmarks`: Used to display your bookmarks bar items in the new-tab UI.
- `favicon`: Used to show bookmark icons.
- `https://ice1.somafm.com/*` and `https://ice2.somafm.com/*`: Used to stream SomaFM audio when you enable music playback.

## Data Stored Locally

Prism stores extension settings and content in your browser storage (such as local extension storage/localStorage), including:

- Widget layout and visibility preferences
- Theme and wallpaper settings
- Timer and countdown state
- Quick note and todo entries
- Audio preferences (for ambient/mixer settings)

This data is stored locally in your browser profile to provide extension functionality.

## Network Requests and Third Parties

Prism may connect to third-party services only to provide user-requested features:

- **SomaFM (`ice1.somafm.com`, `ice2.somafm.com`)** for music streaming in the audio widget.
- **Google favicon service (`www.google.com/s2/favicons`)** as a fallback for bookmark icons (request includes bookmark domain/hostname, not your full bookmark list upload).
- **Image hosts (for selected wallpaper URLs)** when the browser loads remote wallpaper images.

Prism itself does not send your notes, todo text, or extension preference data to a Prism-owned server.

## Data Sharing

Prism does not sell or rent your personal information.  
Data is only shared with third-party services as described above when those features are used.

## Data Retention and Deletion

Your Prism data remains in your browser storage until you remove it. You can delete data by:

- Clearing extension/browser storage,
- Resetting extension settings (if provided in UI), or
- Uninstalling the extension.

## Children's Privacy

Prism is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Security

Prism follows the browser extension security model and requests minimal permissions. No software can guarantee absolute security, but Prism is designed to reduce unnecessary data exposure.

## Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be reflected by revising the "Effective date" above.

## Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact the publisher through the support channel listed on the extension store page.
